About Katie

Hello! My name is Katie, A San Francisco based self-taught UX professional who LOVE developing UI with HTML, CSS, and Javascript/Jquery.

Experienced in front end development and in end to end UX design, including prototype development and user testing, I consider myself as a UX designer and developer hybrid. "Design like you are right, test like you are wrong" - is a principle I stand by when I design and code. I enjoy learning and strive to keep on challenging myself when it comes to UX engineering.

Outside of work, I am a frequent volunteer at a local non-profit animal rescue who plays video games to relax. I am also a mother to two cats and a dog.
